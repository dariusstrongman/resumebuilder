Free toolkit

Behavioral interview question bank.

40+ real questions organized by the competency they're testing. Practice with the STAR format: Situation, Task, Action, Result. Aim for 4–6 sentences per answer: tight enough to land, long enough to land specifically.

Leadership & influence

01Tell me about a time you had to lead without formal authority. What did you do to get buy-in?
02Describe a moment when you changed a teammate's mind about a technical decision. What was your argument?
03Walk me through a time you took an unpopular stance with senior leadership. What was the outcome?
04Tell me about the largest team or project you've coordinated. How did you keep everyone aligned?
05Describe a time you mentored someone who was struggling. What worked, what didn't?
06Tell me about a time you had to deliver feedback that was hard to hear.
07Describe a moment you set a direction for a team when nobody else was offering one.

Conflict & disagreement

08Tell me about a serious disagreement with a coworker. How did it resolve?
09Describe a time your manager made a call you strongly disagreed with. What did you do?
10Walk me through a cross-team conflict you had to broker. What did each side want?
11Tell me about a time a stakeholder pushed for a feature you thought was wrong. How did you handle it?
12Describe a moment you had to deliver bad news to a customer or partner.
13Tell me about a time someone gave you tough feedback. How did you act on it?

Failure & setback

14Tell me about your biggest professional failure. What did you learn?
15Describe a project that missed its deadline or budget. What would you do differently?
16Walk me through a decision that turned out to be wrong. How did you recover?
17Tell me about a time a project you owned was canceled. How did you react?
18Describe a moment you got negative feedback on something you thought was great work.
19Tell me about a time you had to admit you didn't know something to a senior person.

Initiative & ownership

20Tell me about a time you noticed a problem nobody else was solving and fixed it.
21Describe a project you started entirely on your own. What was the result?
22Walk me through a process you saw and improved without being asked.
23Tell me about a time you went beyond your role to help the company.
24Describe a moment you took on something well outside your comfort zone.
25Tell me about an idea you championed despite skepticism. How did it play out?

Teamwork & collaboration

26Tell me about a time you worked with someone whose style was very different from yours.
27Describe the best team you've worked on. What made it work?
28Walk me through how you onboarded into a new team. What did you do in the first 30 days?
29Tell me about a time you had to get help from a team that didn't report to you.
30Describe a moment you sacrificed your own goals to help the team hit theirs.
31Tell me about a remote or distributed team experience. What was hardest?

Pressure, deadlines & ambiguity

32Tell me about the most stressful work moment you've handled. How did you manage?
33Describe a time you had to make a decision with incomplete information.
34Walk me through how you prioritize when everything feels urgent.
35Tell me about a project that required you to learn something new fast. How did you ramp?
36Describe a time you had to push back on an unrealistic deadline.
37Tell me about a moment when scope changed dramatically mid-project.

Customer & impact

38Tell me about a time you went out of your way for a customer. What was the outcome?
39Describe a project where you measured the business impact yourself. What did you learn?
40Walk me through a feature or change you shipped that you're most proud of. Why?
41Tell me about a time you turned an unhappy customer or stakeholder into a champion.
42Describe a moment when data changed your mind about a decision you'd already made.
