Pricing: $1 per tailor vs $29/month for the bundle

Teal+ runs $29/month for unlimited use of the AI resume tailor, cover-letter generator, and tracker. ATSHack charges $1 per tailored resume with no account. If you're applying to one or two openings, $1 is a no-brainer. If you're sending out 20+ tailored resumes a month and want a tracker, Teal might pencil out.

Scope: focused tool vs full job-search platform

Teal bundles a builder, a tracker, a Chrome extension, AI cover letters, LinkedIn analysis, and resume scoring. ATSHack does one thing: tailor a resume to a posting and deliver a PDF + DOCX. We have a free grader, behavioral-question bank, and follow-up email templates as adjacent tools, but the core wedge is fast tailoring.

Source resume: bring your own vs build from scratch

Teal pulls you into their resume builder — you re-enter your work history into their template, then export. ATSHack accepts whatever you already have (PDF, DOCX, plain text) and works from that. If you've spent years polishing your existing resume, ATSHack respects that. If you're starting from scratch, Teal's builder is more guided.

Account requirement

Teal requires an email-based account before you can do anything. ATSHack's $1 path has no account — you upload, paste the posting, pay, and download. If you want a saved dashboard, you upgrade to ATSHack Pro ($15/month or $99/year) and get history + the STAR coach.
