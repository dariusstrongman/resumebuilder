Should you stay in your career, or pivot to a new one?

We compare three paths side by side with real numbers, not vibes. Stay where you are. Pivot to a higher-paying adjacent role. Or specialize inside your current role. Then we hand you a 90-day plan to act on whichever one fits.

How we decide

  1. Stay. Your pay 6 years from now if you do nothing, from BLS 2019-2024 historical growth for your role.
  2. Pivot. The best higher-paying adjacent role you already half-qualify for. Exact missing skills, real cert dollars, weeks of study at your pace.
  3. Specialize. A specialization inside your current role with a sourced pay premium (Levels.fyi 2025, IBEW, AACN, PMI, etc.).
  4. 90-day plan. Concrete tasks for month 1, 2, 3 — checkable from your account. We email you at day 30, 60, 90 to keep score.
