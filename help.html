Pro features

A 4-path diagnostic (Stay, Advance, Specialize, or Pivot) based on your resume and a 7-question intake. You get a verdict with sourced reasons (Mobley turnover model), a 6-month / 1-year / 3-year pay projection, a week-by-week phase breakdown, and a 90-day plan with email check-ins. Built for Pro users actively making a career decision.
STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result) is the structure recruiters score behavioral answers on. Pro gets you five questions tailored to a target role, you answer in your own words, and AI grades each answer on STAR structure: what is strong, what is missing, what to tighten before the interview.
Drafts a tactful, recruiter-friendly follow-up after an interview, recruiter screen, or post-application silence. You pick the situation, paste any context, and get a draft you can edit and send.
A dashboard for every job you've applied to: company, role, status, dates, notes, optional URL. Sends follow-up reminders so applications don't slip through the cracks. Lives on your Account page.
