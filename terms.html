2. What ATSHack does

ATSHack is software that helps you tailor a resume to a specific job posting, grade an existing resume against a posting, and decide on next career moves. Output is generated by AI. It is a writing aid, not professional career or legal advice.

  • Free Resume Grader — paste a resume and a job posting, get an ATS-readability score and a 1-100 match grade.
  • Free Career Direction Report — paste a resume, get a sourced pay range, AI displacement risk, and three adjacent roles you likely qualify for.
  • $1 Tailor — one tailored resume + optional cover letter, delivered as PDF and Word files.
