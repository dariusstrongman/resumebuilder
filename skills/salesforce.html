Salesforce on a resume: the keyword + the cloud + the version.

Salesforce is the dominant CRM in enterprise US sales, marketing, and customer service postings. ATS parsers match "Salesforce" as a literal, but recruiters look for the specific cloud used — Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Marketing Cloud, Pardot, Experience Cloud — and the role taken (admin, developer, end user, business analyst).

Sample bullet templates that prove Salesforce

Use these as a structural template — verb, scope, outcome. Replace the bracketed numbers with your own (verifiable) figures.

  • Administered Salesforce Sales Cloud for a 60-person sales org; built 14 reports, 8 dashboards, and 22 workflow rules used daily.
  • Owned the Salesforce Service Cloud rollout for a 24-person CS team; reduced average ticket-resolution time from 18 hours to 4.5 hours.
  • Wrote 9 Apex triggers and 12 Flow automations for a complex lead-routing process; automated work that previously consumed 14 hours/week of ops time.
