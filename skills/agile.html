Agile on a resume: the methodology, the framework, the role you played.

Agile is the most-used keyword in software, IT, and product postings. ATS parsers match the literal "Agile" but the deeper signal is the framework (Scrum, Kanban, SAFe, LeSS), the role (Scrum Master, Product Owner, team member), and the scale (one squad, multiple squads, full enterprise transformation).

Sample bullet templates that prove Agile

Use these as a structural template — verb, scope, outcome. Replace the bracketed numbers with your own (verifiable) figures.

  • Ran 2-week Scrum sprints across 4 squads (28 engineers); cut time-to-production from 90 days to 11 days and improved on-time delivery from 62% to 91%.
  • Coached 6 squads through a SAFe 6.0 transformation over 9 months; lifted velocity 38% and reduced cross-squad dependency conflicts 52%.
  • Owned the Kanban board and WIP limits for a 12-person ops team; reduced average cycle time from 8 days to 3.5 days.
