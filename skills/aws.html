AWS on a resume: which services to list and which to skip.

AWS is the dominant cloud platform in US tech postings. ATS parsers match "AWS" as a literal but recruiters look for the specific services used — EC2, S3, Lambda, RDS, ECS/EKS, CloudFront, IAM, VPC. Listing 30 services is padding; listing the 6–8 you've used in production is signal.
