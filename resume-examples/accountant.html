Common ATS pitfalls for accountant resumes

Accountant resumes often hide systems experience inside bullets ("managed AP through our ERP"). Move the literal system name (NetSuite, QuickBooks, SAP) into a Skills section AND use it in bullets. ATS parsers are case-sensitive on some certification strings (CPA vs C.P.A.) so use the recruiter's preferred spelling.
