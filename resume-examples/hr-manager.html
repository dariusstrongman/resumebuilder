Common ATS pitfalls for hr manager resumes

HR resumes often list compliance areas ("FLSA, ADA, FMLA") without showing them in action. Pair each compliance keyword with a verb ("investigated 12 ER cases under Title VII," "administered FMLA leave for 28 employees over 14 months"). ATS parsers reward both the keyword presence and the sentence-level context.
