Common ATS pitfalls for sales representative resumes

Sales resumes fail when they describe activities ("prospected, qualified, closed") instead of numbers ("142% of $1.4M quota; closed 14 net-new logos worth $2.1M ARR"). Every role line needs quota + attainment + at least one deal-size proof point. Without those, the ATS still scores keywords but the recruiter throws it out anyway.
