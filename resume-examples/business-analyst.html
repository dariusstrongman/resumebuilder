Common ATS pitfalls for business analyst resumes

BA resumes often read like a list of methods ("requirements gathering, process mapping, stakeholder interviews") without naming the business problem solved. Recruiters and hiring managers want to see the outcome — "cut close cycle from 9 to 5.5 days" — not just the method.
