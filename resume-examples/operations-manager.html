Common ATS pitfalls for operations manager resumes

Ops resumes typically describe activities ("managed operations, oversaw warehouse, led teams") without quantifying scope. Add a one-line Scope statement on every role: "Scope: $14M P&L, 78 FTEs, 2 sites, 3 shifts." Recruiters scan for that line and ATS parsers index it as a structured block.
