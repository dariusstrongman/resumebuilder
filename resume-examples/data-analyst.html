Common ATS pitfalls for data analyst resumes

Data analyst resumes typically over-list tools and under-quantify impact. Listing 14 BI tools in a Skills section is fine; what's missing is the business number — "reduced churn by 6 points," "saved finance 18 hours/week," "drove $1.4M ARR change." The numbers don't have to be huge, but they have to exist.
